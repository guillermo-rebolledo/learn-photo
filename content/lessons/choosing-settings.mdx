## Choose settings from the photograph you want

Begin with the **Photographic Intention**, not a memorized combination. Choose the Exposure Control that most directly shapes it: shutter speed for motion, aperture for depth of field, or a motion-safe shutter and workable aperture before ISO in low light.

Then read the Available Light and Scene Assumptions, make that defining choice, and rebalance the other controls. Check the Rendered Result against each Success Criterion. A tradeoff is information, not a grade, and more than one settings combination can satisfy the same intention.

The Capstone below removes most guidance. Each Challenge keeps its hint hidden until you ask for it, accepts retries, and completes only when every essential Success Criterion is Achieved.
