# Require transparent curriculum sources

The MVP cannot rely on a professional curriculum reviewer, so every Lesson will expose a collapsed “Sources and further reading” section and the Reference will consolidate those Curriculum Sources. Technical claims should use authoritative manufacturer, film-maker, textbook, or institutional material, nuanced claims require an independent cross-check, and Challenges will link to their supporting Lesson rather than repeat citations; this transparency reduces but does not claim to replace future subject-matter review.
